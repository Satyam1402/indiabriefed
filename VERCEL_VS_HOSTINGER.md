# 🤔 Vercel vs Hostinger - Which Should You Choose?

## 🎯 Quick Answer

**Choose Vercel if:**
- ✅ You want FREE hosting
- ✅ You want auto-deployment (Git push = live)
- ✅ You want fastest performance (global CDN)
- ✅ You're comfortable with GitHub
- ✅ You want modern, professional setup

**Choose Hostinger if:**
- ✅ You already paid for Hostinger
- ✅ You prefer traditional hosting
- ✅ You want everything in one place
- ✅ You're not comfortable with Git/GitHub
- ✅ You prefer manual control
